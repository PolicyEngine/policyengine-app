[**_Experience the visualization here._**](../obbba-household-by-household)

## A New Way to Understand Policy Impact

While our [OBBBA Household Explorer](../obbba-household-explorer) provides detailed analysis tools for researchers and policy professionals, OBBBA, Household by Household offers something different: an intuitive, visual journey through the data that anyone can understand and explore.

As you scroll through the experience, you'll see how the OBBBA's provisions — from extended TCJA tax cuts to new exemptions for tips and overtime — create a complex landscape of winners and losers across different income levels.

## What Makes This Tool Special

### 🌟 Immersive Visualization

Watch as thousands of dots representing real households animate across your screen, each positioned by their income level and the percentage change in their net income under OBBBA.

![Screenshot showing the full scatter plot with thousands of household dots, color-coded by impact](obbba-household-by-household-scatter.png)

*Each dot represents a household. Green dots gain under OBBBA, gray dots lose. Hover or click to explore individual stories.*

### 📖 Scroll-Based Storytelling

The visualization guides you through different income groups, revealing patterns and insights as you progress:

- **Lower-income households** (below $50k): See how tax cuts interact with benefit changes
- **Middle-income households** ($50k-$200k): Discover the effects of standard deduction and overtime exemptions
- **Upper-income households** ($200k-$1M): Understand SALT cap and AMT reform impacts
- **Highest-income households** (over $1M): Explore the largest absolute changes

![Screenshot of the middle-income section with narrative text and highlighted households](obbba-household-by-household-middle-income.png)

*As you scroll, the visualization zooms to specific income ranges while explaining key patterns.*

### 🎲 Random Household Spotlights

Each section highlights a randomly selected representative household, complete with:

- Demographics (location, age, dependents)
- Income breakdown
- Specific dollar impact from OBBBA
- Which provisions help or hurt them most

![Screenshot of a household profile card showing detailed impact breakdown](obbba-household-by-household-profile.png)

*Click "Randomize" to explore different households within each income group.*

### 🔄 Compare Baselines

Toggle between two comparison scenarios:

- **TCJA Expiration**: What happens if the 2017 tax cuts expire as scheduled
- **TCJA Extension**: What happens if they're extended (current policy baseline)

This shows how OBBBA's impacts vary depending on what would have happened otherwise.

## Key Insights at a Glance

The visualization reveals several striking patterns:

1. **Widespread but Uneven Impact**: While OBBBA affects over 80% of households, the distribution of gains and losses varies dramatically by income level.
2. **Middle-Class Benefits**: Households earning $50k-$200k see the highest percentage of winners, driven by standard deduction increases and overtime exemptions.
3. **Complex Trade-offs**: Many lower-income households face a mix of tax cuts and benefit reductions, creating both winners and losers in the same income ranges.
4. **Scaling Effects**: The highest earners see the largest absolute dollar changes, both positive and negative, due to provisions like SALT cap modifications.

## Built on PolicyEngine's Enhanced CPS

This visualization uses the same [Enhanced Current Population Survey](https://policyengine.org/us/research/enhanced-cps-beta) dataset that powers all PolicyEngine simulations. By combining multiple data sources with machine learning techniques, we can show impacts on households that collectively represent the entire U.S. population.
